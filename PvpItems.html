Why are Items Important?

Items provide added strength to Loomians which can give an edge in competitive battles. They can help patch a weakness in a Loomian or even provide utility. For Example, Cosmiore Cracked has the ability enchanted coat which reduces the amount of damage taken by Super Effective Moves. Paired with a Rageful Plushie (and Cosmiore Cracked's great Ranged Attack stat), this allows Cosmiore to swap into a Super Effective move, live the hit, set off the Rageful Plushie and gain a massive boost in Ranged Attack.

Another example is Chartiki being able to hold Volcanic Ashes which won't affect Chartiki due it being a part-fire type. However, it can use the move Barter to give the item to a Melee Attacker which cripples its Melee Attacking stat by 50%. It's important that a reasonable justification is given to why an item is given to a Loomian. For example, giving a Shine Pearl to Tiklipse is irrational because Tiklipse's Total Eclipse grants Tiklipse immunity from Light Type attacks (for more information see Set Making and Teambuilding).

Where can I find Items?

Items can be found all across Roria as well as in the Battle Colosseum's Marketplace.
