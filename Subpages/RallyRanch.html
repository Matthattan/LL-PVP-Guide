What is Rally Ranch?

Rally Ranch is located on Route 6 which allows Players to collect new Loomians using their own Loomians that have been given to the Ranch. By default, the Ranch gives you 3 rally spaces to collect Loomians and 1 feed. Currently, all other items can be earned through Trainer Mastery.

As a PVPer, Why should I use this?

With Rallying, Loomians you obtain can get 3 Personalities (known as a Totemised Personality) which means 3 stats of a Loomian are changed instead of 2 personalities (or even one). This is a great advantage for Loomians to utilise as it allows 2 stats to gain a boost or even 1 stat to gain a massive increase.

Another reason to use Rally Ranch is to get Loomians with natural 7x40 UPs: This helps maximise stats that a Loomian may want to take advantage of such as Speed or their dominant Attacking stat.

Many Loomians have access to additional moves through rallying which may be optimal to run. Learning how to give these to your Loomians can give a big advantage in battle.

For more information about learning Rallying moves onto Loomians, See Rally Moves.

What Items does Rally Ranch offer?

Rally Ranch offers Feeds which changes what Unique Points your Rallied Loomians will inherit, Toys which change what personality your Rallied Loomians inherit and Totems that change the type of Personality your Rallied Loomians will obtain.

Rally Feed

Sprite Name Description Obtaining Method
Stromberry Rally Loomians that share at least one Unique Point with each Rallying Loomian. Default
Blumelon Rally Loomians that share at least two Unique Points with each Rallying Loomian. Trainer Mastery level 12
Tutifruit Rally Loomians that share at least three Unique Points with each Rallying Loomian. Trainer Mastery level 22
Wonderbean Rally Loomians that share at least four Unique Points with the Rally Leader. Trainer Mastery level 32
Jewelfruit Rally Loomians that share three to five perfect Unique Points with any of the Rallying Loomians. Trainer Mastery level 78

Rally Toys

Sprite Name Description Obtaining Method
Rubber Toy Rally Loomians that share a personality trait with the Rally Leader or Assistant. Trainer Mastery level 4
Bouncy Ball Rally Loomians that share a positive personality trait with the Rally Leader. Trainer Mastery level 6
Puzzle Cube Rally Loomians that share a positive personality trait with the Rally Assistant. Trainer Mastery level 19
Yo-yo Rally Loomians that have the exact same personality as the Rally Leader. Trainer Mastery level 39

Rally Totems

Sprite Name Description Obtaining Method
Smiling Totem Rally Loomians with two positive personality traits and one strong negative trait. Trainer Mastery level 24
Scowling Totem Rally Loomians with a strong positive personality trait and two negative traits. Trainer Mastery level 26
